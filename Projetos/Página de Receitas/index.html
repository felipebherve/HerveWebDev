Butter Weed

Ingredients

  • Cannabis (decarboxylated)
  • Unsalted butter
BudShop

Preparation method

  • Step 1: Decarboxylation
    1. Preheat your oven to 240°F (115°C).
    2. Break up your cannabis buds into smaller pieces.
    3. Spread the cannabis evenly on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    4. Bake the cannabis in the preheated oven for about 40-45 minutes to activate the cannabinoids through decarboxylation.
    5. Remove the cannabis from the oven and let it cool.


  • Step 2: Butter Infusion
    1. In a saucepan, melt the unsalted butter over low heat.
    2. Once the butter is melted, add the decarboxylated cannabis to the saucepan.
    3. Stir well to combine the cannabis and butter.
    4. Simmer the mixture on low heat for 2-3 hours, stirring occasionally. Make sure the mixture doesn't boil.
    5. The longer you simmer, the stronger the infusion will be. Adjust the time according to your preference.


  • Step 3: Straining and Cooling
    1. Place a fine-mesh strainer or cheesecloth over a heatproof container or bowl.
    2. Slowly pour the mixture through the strainer, allowing the infused butter to strain into the container.
    3. Use a spatula or the back of a spoon to press the remaining butter from the cannabis material.
    4. Let the infused butter cool at room temperature for a while.


  • Step 4: Refrigeration and Storage
    1. Cover the container with a lid or plastic wrap and refrigerate it for a few hours or overnight.
    2. As it cools, the infused butter will solidify and separate from any remaining water.
    3. Once completely solidified, remove the butter from the container and discard any excess water.
    4. Store your cannabis-infused butter in an airtight container in the refrigerator for future use.

Benefits of Cannabis Butter

  • Pain Relief: Cannabis butter contains cannabinoids that can help alleviate chronic pain, inflammation, and muscle soreness.
  • Improved Sleep: Cannabis butter promotes better sleep, making it beneficial for individuals with insomnia or sleep disorders.
  • Enhanced Appetite: Cannabis butter can stimulate the appetite, aiding individuals with conditions like cancer or HIV/AIDS.
  • Reduced Anxiety and Stress: Cannabis butter has calming effects on the mind and body, helping to alleviate symptoms of anxiety and stress.
